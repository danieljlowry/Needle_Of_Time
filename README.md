# Welcome to Needle of Time!

A time traveler is assigned the high-level task of saving the thread of time itself.

However, as the assignment continues on it reveals dark secrets buried in the deep outer reaches of time and space, intented to near see the light day. The entire foundation of time administration, its holier-than-thou director, the ominous time troublemaker, and even the traveler themselves. 

Whether it be reversing the clock into the far past or punching it into the distant future, the player must master each point in time in order to hold all three together!

Will the time traveler be able to vanquish the darkness? 
Or will the darkness encompass the time stream?
Will the director be able to hold every thread together forever?
What secrets were meant to buried?
And which truths were lies all along?

Uncover the secrets of the time stream, its inhabitants, and more!

Happy traveling! And don't let the fragile material of time fragment your being across all of time and space... 

## Controls:

1. A - Move left
2. D - Move right
3. Space - Jump
4. E - time travel to the past (press again to go back to present day)
